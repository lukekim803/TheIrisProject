Welcome to my personal "PROJECT IRIS : Voice of the Wind"

I started doing art around my junior year of highschool and am now a sophmore student studying GAME DESIGN. Finding a passion and love for video games, I've also had the motivation and curiosity of learning how to create them. Understanding how putting smaller things to create greater objects is what gives me joy. Therefore, understanding the small components of game making to create large projects directly reflects on the accomplished journey. Over the past couple of months, I decided to start creating a game. This is just a mere concept which I will build upon. Take on the embarking, spiritual journey while searching for the hidden secrets of nature through the post apocalyptic world. Iris is a third person, rogue-like game with a compelling story. Play as Iris as she explores the beauty of nature's light and having to venture into the DARKEST OF DEPTHS. "Where there is light, the darkness follows, welcome to the IRIS PROJECT."
